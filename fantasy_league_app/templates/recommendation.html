⛳ Fantasy Golf Platform

Product Roadmap & Feature Recommendations

30 Strategic Features to Drive Revenue, Engagement & Growth
🔥 High Priority - Revenue & Engagement
1. Live Scoring Push NotificationsPartially Implemented
You have the infrastructure, but enhance it:
  • Shot-by-shot alerts for your picked players
  • Leaderboard position changes with custom thresholds
  • Eagle/Birdie/Hole-in-one notifications
  • Critical moments (player on 18th hole in contention)
Why: Keeps users engaged throughout the tournament, drives app opens
2. Player Performance Analytics & Insights
New features to add:
  • Course history for each player
  • Recent form (last 5 tournaments)
  • Head-to-head comparisons
  • Strokes gained statistics breakdown
  • Weather impact analysis
Why: Helps users make informed picks, increases perceived value
3. In-App Wallet & Instant Withdrawals
You have Stripe integrated, but add:
  • User balance display
  • Instant withdrawal to bank account
  • Transaction history
  • Bonus credits system
Why: Reduces friction, increases trust, enables promotions
4. Social Features & Rivalries
Tables to add:
  • Friendships/Following system
  • Private chat within leagues
  • Trash talk board
  • Head-to-head challenges
  • Share picks on social media
Why: Social proof, viral growth, increased retention
💰 Revenue Generators
5. Multiple Entry Types
Expand beyond 3-player picks:
  • Pick 6 Format (higher entry fees)
  • Salary Cap Format (budget management)
  • Survivor Pools (pick one player per week)
  • Props Bets (will there be a hole-in-one?)
  • Daily Fantasy (single-day contests)
Why: Caters to different player types, increases ARPU
6. Premium Features / Subscription
Premium tier ($9.99/month):
  • Advanced statistics
  • Expert picks & analysis
  • Early access to leagues
  • Custom league branding
  • Priority support
  • Ad-free experience
Why: Recurring revenue, higher LTV
7. Affiliate Program for Clubs
Add to Club model:
  • referral_code
  • total_referrals
  • commission_earned
  • payout_threshold
Track club referrals, give commission on referred user entries
Why: User acquisition, club engagement, word-of-mouth growth
📊 User Experience Enhancements
8. Enhanced Player Selection Interface
  • Filters: Odds range, form, course history, nationality
  • Favorites system (star players for quick access)
  • Comparison tool (side-by-side stats)
  • AI recommendations based on user history
  • Undo last pick before deadline
Why: Reduces decision fatigue, improves UX
9. Live Leaderboard Enhancements
Add real-time features:
  • Projected final positions (based on current scores)
  • "You need X under to win" calculator
  • Animated position changes
  • Virtual ticker showing all league updates
  • "Watch mode" for specific players
Why: Increases engagement, keeps users in app longer
10. Tournament Center / Hub
Central page for each tournament with:
  • Live scoring
  • Course map with hole-by-hole breakdown
  • Weather conditions
  • Field strength analysis
  • Historical tournament data
  • Video highlights (YouTube API)
Why: Makes your app the go-to destination, not just entry platform
🎮 Gamification & Retention
11. Achievement System (Expand Current)
You have basic achievements, add more:
  • Streaks: Win 3 weekends in a row
  • Course Master: Win same tournament 3 times
  • Bracket Buster: Pick underdog winner
  • Perfect Pick: All 3 players finish top 10
  • Iron Man: Enter 20 consecutive tournaments
  • Display badges on profile
  • Leaderboard for achievement points
Why: Drives engagement, creates completionist users
12. Seasonal Leaderboards & Championships
Track points across entire season, award championship prizes. Example Season model with name, start/end dates, and championship prize fields.
Why: Long-term engagement, gives losing users reason to keep playing
13. Tutorial & Onboarding Flow
  • Interactive tutorial for first-time users
  • Demo league with fake money
  • "Recommended for beginners" leagues
  • In-app tips and strategy guides
  • Video tutorials
Why: Reduces churn, increases activation rate
📱 Mobile-Specific Features
14. Offline Mode
  • Cache league standings
  • View past results offline
  • Queue picks when offline, sync when online
Why: Better mobile experience, works in poor connectivity
15. Apple/Google Wallet Integration
  • Add league entry pass to wallet
  • Tournament countdown timer
  • Quick access from lock screen
Why: Increases visibility, reduces forgotten entries
🤖 Advanced/AI Features
16. AI-Powered Pick Assistant
ML model features to predict player performance based on:
  • Recent form
  • Course history
  • Weather conditions
  • Playing partners
  • Time of day tee time
  • Generate optimal lineups
  • Risk/reward analysis
Why: Helps casual users compete, premium feature potential
17. Smart Alerts & Recommendations
  • "Your player is about to tee off"
  • "Similar users are picking [player]"
  • "League deadline in 2 hours - spots filling fast"
  • "Your friend just joined a league"
Why: FOMO, drives conversions
18. Predictive Analytics Dashboard
Show users:
  • Win probability based on current scores
  • "If X shoots Y, you'll finish in Z position"
  • Historical data: "Players in your position won 23% of the time"
  • Monte Carlo simulations for final standings
Why: Engaging, keeps users checking app
🏆 Club-Specific Features
19. Club Management Portal (Enhance Current)
You have basics, add:
  • Member management (invite, remove, roles)
  • Club statistics (most successful members)
  • Club leaderboards (across all club leagues)
  • Automated weekly leagues (recurring setup)
  • Custom branding (club logo, colors)
  • Email templates for league invitations
  • Club chat/bulletin board
Why: Makes clubs stickier, increases club retention
20. White Label Solution
Allow larger clubs to have their own branded version:
  • Custom domain (fantasygolf.clubname.com)
  • Custom branding throughout
  • Their own payment processing
  • Higher revenue share
Why: B2B revenue, larger contracts, stability
📈 Analytics & Business Intelligence
21. User Analytics Dashboard
For site admins:
  • User cohort analysis
  • Retention metrics
  • Revenue per user
  • Most popular leagues
  • Churn prediction
  • A/B testing framework
Why: Data-driven decisions, optimize conversions
22. Export & Reporting Tools
For clubs:
  • League results export (PDF, Excel)
  • Financial reports
  • Tax documents (Form 1099 for US users)
  • Member activity reports
Why: Professionalism, reduces support burden
🔐 Trust & Safety
23. Enhanced Security Features
  • Two-factor authentication
  • Biometric login (Face ID, fingerprint)
  • Suspicious activity detection
  • Account recovery flow
  • Session management (view active sessions)
Why: Trust, prevents account takeover, regulatory compliance
24. Responsible Gaming Features
Add to User model: daily/weekly spend limits, self-exclusion, reality check frequency
  • Deposit limits
  • Time limits
  • Self-exclusion options
  • Reality checks ("You've been playing for 2 hours")
Why: Regulatory compliance, ethical, builds trust
🌍 Expansion Features
25. Multi-Language Support
  • Spanish, French, German initially
  • Based on user's club location
  • Different odds formats (decimal, fractional, American)
Why: International expansion, larger market
26. Multi-Tournament Support
Allow users to:
  • Enter multiple tournaments simultaneously
  • Cross-tournament leaderboards
  • "Fantasy FedEx Cup" season-long format
Why: Increased engagement, more entry opportunities
27. Corporate Leagues
B2B offering for companies:
  • Team-building tool
  • Corporate branding
  • Bulk user management
  • Invoice billing (not credit card)
  • Client entertainment packages
Why: Larger contracts, stable revenue, new market
🎁 Marketing & Growth
28. Referral Program
Add to User model: referral_code, referred_by_id, referral_bonus_earned, referrals_count

Give users credit for referring friends:
  • €5 credit for referrer and referee
  • Track viral coefficient
  • Leaderboard for top referrers
Why: Viral growth, lower CAC
29. Free-to-Play Option
  • Free leagues with smaller prizes
  • Sponsored by advertisers
  • "Freemium" model
Why: Lower barrier to entry, larger funnel
30. Email Marketing Automation
  • Welcome series
  • Weekly tournament preview
  • "You haven't played in a while" re-engagement
  • Win/loss follow-ups
  • Personalized recommendations
Why: Retention, reactivation, CLV increase
