Building Your Dream Team

When selecting your 3 golfers, keep these tips in mind:

  • Mix favorites and underdogs
    Balance star players with potential breakout performances
  • Check recent form
    Look at players' recent tournament results and course history
  • Watch the odds limits
    Some leagues restrict how many favorites you can pick
  • Don't forget the tiebreaker
    Your tiebreaker guess could determine the winner!
