Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Fantasy Fairways collects, uses, and protects your personal data in accordance with EU data protection laws, including GDPR.

1. Data We Collect

For Club Admins & Users

We collect your name, email address, and organisation name (for clubs) to manage your account.

For League Participants

We collect your name and email address to register you for the leagues you join.

Usage & Device Data

We collect your IP address, browser type, and pages visited to improve and secure our services.

2. How We Use Your Data

To Provide Our Service

Your data is essential for account registration, league management, billing, and platform support.

To Improve Our Platform

We analyze usage data for security, fraud prevention, and to enhance the user experience.

3. Data Sharing & Security

Third-Party Services

We share necessary data with trusted partners like Stripe for payment processing and with our hosting providers.

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security, including encryption and access controls, to protect your data.

4. Your Rights

Access, Correct, or Delete

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal data. You can also object to our data processing or request a copy of your data.

Contact Us

To exercise any of these rights, please get in touch with our support team. info@fantasyfairways.ie
